The Music Competition
Today is the day of the big school competition. Mia and her friends are in the hallway. Their teacher, Mr. Chen, looks at them and says, "Everyone, you have to wait patiently in line. The judges are listening to the other group now. You can't make any noise because it will scare the performers. Mia, you are wearing your uniform neatly, which is great! When you go on stage, you should breathe deeply if you feel nervous. Sing clearly so the people at the back can hear you. You shouldn't worry too much. Just do your best!" Mia nods and waits for her turn.

Safety in the Safari Park
Luke is visiting a safari park with his family. The guide gives everyone a list of rules. "The animals here are amazing, but they are not pets," the guide explains. "You have to stay inside the car at all times. You can't open the windows to touch the rhinos. If you see a lion, you should watch quietly. You shouldn't shout loudly because it might make the animals angry. Some animals move slowly, but they are very strong. Also, you have to throw your litter into the garbage cans to keep the park clean. If we follow these rules, we will have a safe trip."
