英文語法特訓：Is vs Does 強化篇 (Retest 2)

📚 學習重點回顧：
1. Do / Does 是助動詞，用來詢問「動作」。句子裡一定要有動詞原形。
2. Is / Are 是繫動詞，用來詢問「狀態」。句子裡通常是形容詞、名詞或地點。
3. 記得 Does 是小偷，會偷走動詞的 s！

Part A: Fill in the Blanks (Is, Are, Do, Does)

1. ________ your brother play basketball after school?

2. ________ the supermarket closed on Mondays?

3. ________ we having pizza for dinner tonight?

4. ________ the bus arrive every ten minutes?

5. ________ these apples sweet and juicy?

6. ________ you brush your teeth every morning?

Part B: Find and Fix the Mistake

7. When is the movie start? (改寫正確句子)

8. Does your house big? (改寫正確句子)

9. Does the children play in the park? (改寫正確句子)

10. Is she speak English well? (改寫正確句子)

Part C: Sentence Transformation

11. The cat sleeps on the sofa. (改為問句)

12. The water is hot. (改為問句)

13. The birds sing in the morning. (改為問句)
