The Power of Iceland
Iceland is a unique island in the North Atlantic. It is famous for its natural beauty, including hot springs and massive glaciers. Because Iceland has many volcanoes, the ground is very hot. This heat is used to create "geothermal energy." People in Iceland use this hot water from the ground to heat their homes and swimming pools. They also use rushing water from waterfalls to make electricity. By using the wind, the sun, and the earth, Iceland shows the world how to live without using dirty fuels like oil or coal.

2. What makes the ground in Iceland hot?
3. What do people use to heat their swimming pools?

A Visit to the Doctor
Yesterday, Sam did not go to school because he felt sick. He had a high fever and a very sore throat. When he swallowed his food, it felt painful. His mother took him to see Dr. Miller.
